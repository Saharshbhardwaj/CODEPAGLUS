Art

Uttar Pradesh is known for its rich art forms like Chikankari embroidery, Zardozi, and Mughal miniature paintings.

Music

The state has given birth to Hindustani classical music and legendary musicians like Tansen and Ravi Shankar.

Dance

Kathak, one of the eight classical dance forms of India, has deep roots in Uttar Pradesh.

Festivals

Festivals like Diwali in Ayodhya, Holi in Barsana, and the Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj attract visitors from all over the world.

Cuisine

The state is famous for its Awadhi and Mughlai cuisine, including dishes like kebabs, biryani, and sweets like peda and jalebi.
